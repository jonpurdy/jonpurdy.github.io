How to plan, estimate, and build an MVP with Scrum

2020-09-30

I now have two pages in my PM*Framework.

How to Plan and Estimate an MVP: Explains how to figure out what you want to build, break it down into User Stories, and estimate with reasonable accuracy how long it will take to build.

How to build an MVP with Scrum: Goes through the steps of building an MVP with Scrum, including Sprint Planning, Sprint Review and Retrospective, adjusting estimates, and how to support development teams during each Sprint.

I have the PM*Framework as a separate topic at the top rather than pages as blog posts since they are intended to be continuously updated with additional learning and information. Please have a look and let me know if you have any feedback or suggestions! I'm on Twitter @jonpurdy.
